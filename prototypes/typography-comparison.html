Typography Notes

  • Geist — Vercel's font, extremely Linear-like. Clean, professional, great for data-heavy UIs. Free via CDN.
  • Plus Jakarta Sans — Slightly warmer than Geist, friendly but still professional. Popular with modern SaaS.
  • Manrope — Geometric sans with personality. Works well at all sizes. Slightly more distinctive.
  • Outfit — Clean geometric with subtle character. Good for headlines and body text alike.
  • All mono fonts pair well with their sans counterparts. JetBrains Mono has the best ligatures for code.
